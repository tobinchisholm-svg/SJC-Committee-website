Welcome to St. Joseph's Parish

Port Hawkesbury, Nova Scotia


Whether you are joining us for Mass, viewing our livestreamed celebrations, or visiting our website or Facebook page, we extend to you a warm and heartfelt welcome.

In our journey of faith, we strive to preserve our faith traditions, while working together to develop and maintain a parish community that is active, vibrant, and spiritually strong contributing to a society that promotes the peace and love of God and one another.

It is our sincere hope that you will come to regard our parish as your spiritual home, or a spiritual home away from home. As a devoted Roman Catholic community of faith, we are committed to serving all those entrusted to our care and to leading others to Christ.

We invite you to explore our website and to join us for one of our Eucharistic celebrations. We look forward to welcoming you in person.

May God's blessing be upon you and upon those whom you hold dear.
