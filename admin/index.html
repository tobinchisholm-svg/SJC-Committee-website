📋 Admin Help Guide

Bulletins

Upload the new weekly bulletin. Add a title (e.g. "June 22, 2026 Bulletin"), upload the two bulletin images, and click Publish. The old bulletin will be automatically removed. Only the most recent bulletin shows on the website.

Announcements

Add any parish announcements here. You can include a title, text, and an image or PDF. Click Publish and it will appear on the homepage immediately.

Calendar Events

Add events to the parish calendar. Fill in the event name, date and time, and any details. Click Publish and it will appear as a dot on the calendar on the website. Visitors can click the date to see the event details.

Parish Gallery

Upload photos to the homepage photo gallery. Add a caption and upload the photo. Click Publish and it will appear in the gallery carousel on the homepage.

For technical support contact Tobin.

💡 Tips & Troubleshooting

Editing an Entry

To edit something already published, just click on it in the list, make your changes, and hit Publish again. It will update on the website automatically.

Deleting an Entry

Open the entry you want to remove, click the arrow next to the Publish button, and select Delete. The item will be removed from the website within 30–60 seconds.

Something Not Showing Up?

After clicking Publish, wait 30–60 seconds for the website to update. If it still doesn't appear, try refreshing the page. If the problem continues, contact Tobin.

Deleting Calendar Events

After deleting a calendar event, the dot will disappear from the website automatically. If the calendar disappears entirely, it will come back on its own within a minute.
