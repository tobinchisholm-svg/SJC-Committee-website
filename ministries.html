Parish Ministries

Serving our community through faith and dedication

Music Ministry

The Music Ministry is a cherished part of our parish worship. Through music, it enriches the liturgy, lifts hearts in prayer, and brings our community together in praise. Hymns, psalms, and other sacred music help create a joyful and reverent spirit during Mass and other liturgical celebrations.

This ministry includes choir members, instrumentalists, and other musicians who generously share their gifts to lead the congregation in song. They select music that reflects the liturgical season and supports the readings and prayers. Members gather regularly to prepare for Sunday Mass, feast days, and sacramental celebrations such as weddings, funerals, and confirmations.

If you have a love for music and feel called to share your gifts in worship, we warmly invite you to join the Music Ministry. Whether you sing, play an instrument, or simply wish to be part of this ministry, there is a place for you.

Contact the Parish Office

Eucharist Ministry

The Eucharist Ministers serve a vital role in our parish by assisting the priest in distributing the Body and Blood of Christ to the faithful during Mass. This ministry is a profound expression of love and service, as it allows parishioners to help ensure that the Eucharist, the source and summit of our faith, is shared reverently and efficiently within our community.

As a Eucharist Minister you will have the privilege of not only helping during Mass but also, in some cases, bringing the Eucharist to those who are homebound, in hospitals, or in care facilities. This act of charity extends the love and presence of Christ to those who are unable to join us in person, ensuring that they remain connected to the life of the Church and receive spiritual nourishment.

For more information about joining the Eucharist Ministry, please contact the parish office.

Contact the Parish Office

Lector Ministry

The Lector Ministry is a vital part of our parish's liturgical life, providing an opportunity for parishioners to proclaim the Word of God during Mass. Lectors have the important responsibility of reading Scripture passages from the Old and New Testaments, delivering these sacred texts in a clear and engaging manner so that the congregation can reflect deeply on God's message.

Through their ministry, lectors help to make the Word of God come alive for those who are gathered in worship.

For more information about joining the Lector Ministry, please contact the parish office.

Contact the Parish Office

Hospitality Ministry

The Hospitality Team plays a vital role in fostering a warm and welcoming environment within our parish community. The members of this ministry are dedicated to creating opportunities for fellowship and connection among parishioners, helping to build a sense of belonging and togetherness. The Hospitality Team ensures that all who come to worship, celebrate, and participate in parish events feel welcomed, valued, and supported.

Volunteers in the Hospitality Team assist with organizing and hosting various parish events and social gatherings. They help set up, serve refreshments, greet attendees, and create a friendly and inviting atmosphere. Their efforts provide a space for building relationships and strengthening the bonds of our faith community.

If you enjoy making others feel welcome and have a heart for service, consider joining the Hospitality Team.

Contact the Parish Office

Greeters and Ushers

The Greeters and Ushers Ministry is an essential part of creating a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere at our parish. Greeters and ushers serve as the first point of contact for parishioners and visitors attending Mass, making everyone feel welcome and ensuring that each person is greeted with warmth and kindness. Their role is crucial in fostering a sense of belonging and community within our church.

If you have a friendly and approachable demeanour and a heart for hospitality, we encourage you to join the Greeters and Ushers Ministry. Your presence and kindness can make a significant difference in helping people feel at home in our parish community.

Contact the Parish Office

Funeral Servers Ministry

Funeral servers assist the priest and grieving families by setting up for the liturgy, preparing the altar, and serving during the Mass. They play a vital role in creating a prayerful atmosphere that honours the deceased and offers comfort to those who mourn.

Volunteers in this ministry are trained to perform various tasks, such as carrying the cross, holding candles, assisting with incense, and guiding the congregation during the funeral rites.

If you have a compassionate heart and a desire to support families during their time of loss, we invite you to consider joining the Funeral Servers Ministry.

Contact the Parish Office
