St. Joseph's Closet

Caring for each other

About the Closet

St. Joseph's Closet is an initiative by St. Joseph's Parish aimed at providing clothing and essential items to those in need. By creating a welcoming environment for individuals and families experiencing difficulties, this parish charity seeks to promote dignity and respect.

Volunteers are essential in the process of collecting, sorting, and distributing donations, making sure that everyone can access the resources they require. Through this compassionate mission, St. Joseph's Closet aims to make a positive difference and support local communities.

Hours

10:00 AM – 3:00 PM

What We Offer

  • Clothing
  • Footwear
  • Bedding
  • Housewares
  • Seasonal Items

Get Involved

Volunteers are always welcome. Whether you'd like to donate items or help with sorting and distribution, contact the parish office to find out how you can help.

Donations Welcome

St. Joseph's Closet accepts donations of gently used clothing, footwear, bedding, housewares, and seasonal items. Drop-offs can be arranged by contacting the parish office.
