Dedicated volunteers serving our parish community

Parish Pastoral Council

Chairperson Cathy MacDonald

The Parish Pastoral Council is a collaborative leadership group that works closely with the pastor to provide guidance and direction for the spiritual and pastoral needs of our parish community. The council serves as a consultative body, offering insight, advice, and support to help shape the vision and mission of the parish. Its members are dedicated to fostering a vibrant, faith-filled community where all parishioners feel welcome, supported, and encouraged in their spiritual journey.

Members of the Parish Pastoral Council meet regularly to discuss various aspects of parish life, including liturgy, faith formation, social outreach, and community building. They help identify the needs of the parish, develop goals and plans to address those needs, and work to ensure that the parish remains a dynamic and welcoming place for all. The council also plays a key role in coordinating with other parish ministries, promoting collaboration and unity within the parish.

If you have a passion for leadership and a commitment to serving your parish community, we invite you to consider joining the Parish Pastoral Council. Please contact the parish office for more information.

Finance Committee

Chairperson Kevin MacEachern

The Parish Finance Committee plays a critical role in supporting the stewardship and financial health of our parish community. This ministry is entrusted with advising the pastor on the prudent management of the parish's financial resources, ensuring that our funds are used effectively to support our mission, ministries, and outreach efforts.

Members of the Finance Committee work together to review budgets, oversee financial reporting, plan for future needs, and develop strategies to maintain and grow the financial stability of the parish. By offering their expertise in financial management, fundraising, and planning, committee members help ensure that the parish is able to fulfill its spiritual, pastoral, and community responsibilities.

For more information about the Finance Committee, please contact the parish office.

Fundraising Committee

Chairperson Louise Campbell

The Fundraising Committee works to support the financial needs of the parish through creative and community-focused initiatives. Their efforts help fund parish projects, improvements, and outreach programs that benefit both parishioners and the wider community.

Liturgy Committee

Chairperson Marina Martens

The Liturgy Committee works to ensure that the celebration of Mass and other sacramental occasions are prayerful, reverent, and enriching for all parishioners. They coordinate the various liturgical ministries and help plan special celebrations throughout the liturgical year.

For more information about the Liturgy Committee, please contact the parish office.

Property Committee

Chairperson Sandra Praught

The Property Committee manages the physical infrastructure of our parish and plans for its future. They oversee routine maintenance of our parish buildings as well as identify long-term improvement needs. They are responsible for the rental of parish spaces for functions, meetings, and support groups.

Whether you want to join the committee, help out with an upcoming project, or report a maintenance concern on the property, please contact the Committee Chair or the parish office.

902-625-1045

Cemetery Committee

Chairperson Gelas Boudreau

The Cemetery Committee is a dedicated group of parish volunteers appointed to oversee the care, maintenance, and long-term preservation of both parish cemeteries. Their mission is to ensure our cemeteries remain a sacred, peaceful, and dignified resting place for our deceased community members while offering compassionate support to their families.

The committee meets 5–6 times per year and welcomes new volunteers who wish to share their time or expertise in landscaping, administration, or historical research.

For more information about the Cemetery Committee or to volunteer, please contact the parish office.
